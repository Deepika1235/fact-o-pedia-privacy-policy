Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how the Fact-O-Pedia app handles user data and information.

1. Data Collection

Fact-O-Pedia does not collect or store any personal information such as name, email, phone number, or location.

2. Permissions

The app may request access to the following:

  • Camera: For image-based fact checking using OCR (Optical Character Recognition).
  • Microphone: For voice-based search queries.
  • Internet: To fetch fact-checking data and verify news from public APIs.

3. Third-Party Services

Fact-O-Pedia may use third-party services like Firebase, Google APIs, and machine learning libraries to enhance functionality. These services may collect limited usage data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

4. Children's Privacy

Our app is not specifically directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

5. Security

We prioritize your privacy and do not store or transmit personal data. All processing is done locally or via secure, trusted APIs.

6. Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be posted on this page.
